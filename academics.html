Academic Excellence at Angkor High School: Nurturing Minds, Shaping Futures


Located amidst the historic city of Siem Reap, Cambodia, Angkor High School stands as a beacon of academic excellence, offering students a rigorous and enriching educational experience. With a commitment to fostering intellectual curiosity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning, Angkor High School prepares its students to excel in the global community.

Comprehensive Curriculum

At Angkor High School, the cornerstone of academic success lies in its comprehensive curriculum. Designed to meet the highest standards of excellence, the curriculum encompasses a wide range of subjects spanning the arts, sciences, humanities, and vocational studies. From mathematics and literature to biology and computer science, students at Angkor High School are exposed to a diverse array of disciplines, providing them with a well-rounded education that prepares them for success in college and beyond.


Dedicated Faculty

Central to the academic success of Angkor High School is its team of dedicated and highly qualified faculty members. Comprising experienced educators and subject matter experts, the faculty at Angkor High School are committed to inspiring and empowering students to reach their full potential. Through innovative teaching methods, personalized attention, and a genuine passion for education, teachers at Angkor High School create a dynamic learning environment where students are encouraged to ask questions, think critically, and explore new ideas.


Advanced Placement and Enrichment Programs

Recognizing the importance of academic rigor and intellectual challenge, Angkor High School offers a range of Advanced Placement (AP) and enrichment programs for its students. These programs provide motivated students with the opportunity to engage in college-level coursework and earn college credit while still in high school. Whether it's AP Calculus, AP Literature, or AP Environmental Science, Angkor High School empowers students to pursue their academic interests and push the boundaries of their knowledge, preparing them for success in the competitive world of higher education.


Holistic Assessment and Support

At Angkor High School, academic success is not solely defined by grades and test scores. The school takes a holistic approach to assessment, considering factors such as character, leadership, and personal growth in addition to academic achievement. Furthermore, Angkor High School provides a range of support services to help students thrive academically, including tutoring programs, counseling services, and college guidance resources. By addressing the individual needs of each student and fostering a supportive and nurturing environment, Angkor High School ensures that every student has the opportunity to succeed.
