Joining the Legacy: Admission to Angkor High School






Angkor High School, nestled in the heart of Siem Reap, Cambodia, offers students a transformative educational experience steeped in academic excellence and cultural heritage. Aspiring students from diverse backgrounds are welcomed into the Angkor High School community, where they embark on a journey of discovery, growth, and achievement. Here's a guide to the admissions process for those eager to join the ranks of Angkor High School.








Application Process

The admissions process at Angkor High School begins with the completion of an application form, which can typically be obtained from the administrative office. Prospective students are required to provide basic personal information, academic records, and any additional documentation requested by the school.




Entrance Examination

Once the application is submitted, prospective students may be required to undergo an entrance examination to assess their academic aptitude and readiness for the curriculum at Angkor High School. The examination may cover a range of subjects, including mathematics, language arts, and critical thinking skills.





Interview

Following the entrance examination, shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview with members of the admissions committee. The interview provides an opportunity for the school to learn more about the student's interests, aspirations, and suitability for the academic and extracurricular programs offered at Angkor High School. It also allows prospective students to ask questions and gain insight into the school's culture and community.





Evaluation Criteria

Admission to Angkor High School is based on a holistic evaluation of the student's academic performance, extracurricular involvement, personal character, and potential to contribute positively to the school community. While academic achievement is important, the school also values qualities such as leadership, creativity, and a commitment to service.




Diversity and Inclusivity

Angkor High School is committed to creating a diverse and inclusive learning environment that celebrates differences and fosters mutual respect and understanding among students from various backgrounds. The admissions process is designed to welcome students from diverse cultural, socioeconomic, and academic backgrounds, enriching the educational experience for all members of the school community.



A Journey of Opportunity

In conclusion, admission to Angkor High School offers students the opportunity to embark on a transformative journey of academic excellence, personal growth, and cultural immersion. Through a rigorous admissions process that values diversity, inclusivity, and individual potential, Angkor High School seeks to identify and nurture the next generation of leaders, innovators, and global citizens. For those eager to join the legacy of Angkor High School, the admissions process serves as the gateway to a world of opportunity and possibility, where students are empowered to pursue their passions, realize their potential, and make a positive impact on the world around them.
